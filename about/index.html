Rebecca Hsieh



Hello! My name is Rebecca Hsieh.

I am currently a Software Engineer at Cisco in the San Francisco Bay Area building a multicloud management platform through APIs for multiple microservices along with other backend shenanigans.

When I'm not staring into the depths of my laptop screen or curating my Spotify playlist of the season -- you can catch me either baking (banana bread is my specialty!), knitting, at a gym class, or outside enjoying the city life.

As a womxn in tech growing up in the rougher edges of Seattle who knew nothing about computer science until late into my university career, giving back and working with underprivileged students that are interested in STEAM (science, technology, engineering, arts, mathematics) has always been a huge passion of mine. Please do not hesitate to reach out to me via LinkedIn or e-mail if there are any opportunities or events that I can volunteer at!
